The Functionality

It's like having a tablet in my pocket, and the seamless transition from outer to inner screen is simply magical!

I'm a stickler for fast, smooth performance, and the Fold 4 delivers on that front too. I've never experienced any freezes or lag, even when running multiple apps or tasks at once.
